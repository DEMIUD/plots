Metric Definitions

  • Total Energy: All energy a person commands (food + external)
  • External Energy: Total minus metabolic baseline (~3.6 GJ/yr)
  • Multiplier: How many "body equivalents" of energy each person controls
  • Watts: Continuous power equivalent (easier to intuit than GJ/year)
